⚪ Others

viel adverb much / a lot viel Arbeit (uncountable) / viele Termine (countable plural)
morgen adverb tomorrow At sentence start causes verb inversion: Morgen habe ich…
leider adverb unfortunately Expresses regret. At start causes inversion: Leider habe ich keine Zeit.
dann adverb then / after that Sequence. At start causes inversion: Dann telefoniere ich.
vielleicht adverb maybe / perhaps Possibility. At start causes inversion: Vielleicht hat er Zeit.
schon wieder phrase again (with frustration) Exasperated repetition: Das Passwort ist schon wieder falsch!
