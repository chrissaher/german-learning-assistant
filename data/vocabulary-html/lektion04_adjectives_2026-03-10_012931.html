📚 Vocabulary Reference

schön beautiful / lovely / nice ↔ hässlich False friend alert: NOT related to English "shown". Spanish: bonito/a.
teuer expensive ↔ günstig / billig No English/Spanish cognate. Tip: loses inner -e before endings → ein teures Sofa (not "teueres").
hässlich ugly ↔ schön The -lich suffix is a common German adjective-forming ending (like English -ly/-ish).
günstig cheap / affordable / favorable ↔ teuer More polite register than "billig". The -ig suffix is common in German adjectives.
modern modern ↔ altmodisch True cognate! English: modern. Spanish: moderno/a. Easy to remember.
praktisch practical / handy ↔ unpraktisch Cognate with English "practical" and Spanish "práctico". Also means "convenient/handy".
neu new ↔ alt No strong cognate. Spanish: nuevo/a. English "new" has different pronunciation.
klein small ↔ groß No direct English/Spanish cognate. Spanish: pequeño/a.
groß big / tall ↔ klein Used for both size (big) AND height (tall/large). Spanish: grande.
