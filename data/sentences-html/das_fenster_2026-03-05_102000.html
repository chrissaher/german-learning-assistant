Simple 3 sentences

1.Simple
Das Fenster ist groß.
→ The window is big.
📝 Das Fenster is neuter nominative (subject); predicate adjective groß takes no ending after ist.
2.Simple
Ich öffne das Fenster.
→ I open the window.
📝 Accusative neuter: das Fenster stays das Fenster — neuter nouns look identical in nominative and accusative.
3.Simple
Das ist ein Fenster.
→ That is a window.
📝 Ein Fenster uses the neuter indefinite article; this structure identifies or points to something.
