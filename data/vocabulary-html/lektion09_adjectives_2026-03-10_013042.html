📚 Vocabulary Reference

lecker delicious / tasty ↔ ekelhaft
fit fit / in good shape ↔ unfit
English cognate: 'fit'. Spanish: 'en forma'. Used predicatively at A1: Ich bin fit.
intelligent intelligent ↔ dumm
Cognate with English and Spanish 'inteligente'. German stress: in-te-li-GENT (last syllable).
prima great / excellent ↔ schlecht
⚠️ Spanish false friend: 'prima' in Spanish = female cousin. In German = great/excellent. Indeclinable — never changes form.
perfekt perfect ↔ mangelhaft
Cognate with English 'perfect'. Note German spelling: perfekt (with 'k', no 'ct').
besonders especially / particularly —
Functions as adjective and adverb. As adverb before 'gern': besonders gern = especially like. Never inflects.
