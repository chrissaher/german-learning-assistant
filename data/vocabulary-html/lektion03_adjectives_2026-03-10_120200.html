📚 Vocabulary Reference

verheiratet married ↔ ledig / unverheiratet
geschieden divorced ↔ verheiratet Partizip II of 'scheiden' used as an adjective. Spanish: divorciado/a.
allein alone / single ↔ zusammen English cognate: 'alone' (Germanic root). Used predicatively or adverbially.
richtig correct / right ↔ falsch Not a cognate with English 'right' or Spanish 'correcto'. Must memorize.
falsch wrong / false ↔ richtig Cognate: English 'false', Spanish 'falso' (all from Latin 'falsus').
Key grammar note: Predicative adjectives (used after sein) are NOT inflected in German: Er ist verheiratet. / Sie ist allein. — no endings added. Attributive adjectives (before nouns) DO take endings: die richtige Antwort, ein falsches Bild.
